## 🧭 How a day works (the whole routine)

Each day card looks the same, so you never have to think about *how*, only *do*:

| Part | Icon | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| Goal | 🎯 | One sentence: what today is about |
| Learn | ▶️ | Click the link(s), watch/read (this is your **morning** ~2h) |
| Practice | 🛠️ | Do ONE small hands-on task (this is your **night** ~2h) |
| Checklist | ✅ | Tick 3–5 boxes to prove you did it |
| Log | 📝 | Write ONE line in [`PROGRESS.md`](./PROGRESS.md) |
| Next | ➡️ | See what tomorrow is, then close the laptop |

**Split your 4 hours:** morning = 🎯 + ▶️ (learn). Night = 🛠️ (build/practice).
If you only have 2 hours one day, do the Practice — building beats watching.

---

## 🟢 The 3 rules (that's all)

1. **Do the practice, not just the video.** Watching isn't learning; doing is.
2. **If you don't get it, don't skip it.** Redo it tomorrow. The plan waits for you.
3. **Every bit of code goes to GitHub.** One commit per day, even tiny. It's your journal.
