## 🆓 Best free resources (used where they beat everything else)

### Understanding & intuition
- **3Blue1Brown — Neural Networks series** (YouTube): the best *visual* intuition for
  how neural nets and transformers work. Channel: [@3blue1brown](https://www.youtube.com/@3blue1brown)
- **The Illustrated Transformer** — Jay Alammar: clearest written explainer of
  attention/transformers. [read](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-transformer/)

### Build-from-scratch (the "behind the scenes" gold)
- **Neural Networks: Zero to Hero** — Andrej Karpathy: builds neural nets and a GPT
  from scratch, in code. The single best "what's really happening" resource.
  [course page](https://karpathy.ai/zero-to-hero.html) ·
  [YouTube playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAqhIrjkxbuWI23v9cThsA9GvCAUhRvKZ)

### Python (backup/second explanation)
- **Python for Everybody** — Dr. Chuck (free full site + videos): [py4e.com](https://www.py4e.com/)

### LLMs, agents & the modern stack (with free certificates)
- **Hugging Face LLM Course** (free, certificate): hands-on modern LLMs.
  [start](https://huggingface.co/learn/llm-course/chapter0/1)
- **Hugging Face Agents Course** (free, certificate): build your first agent.
  [start](https://huggingface.co/learn/agents-course/en/unit1/introduction)
- **DeepLearning.AI short courses** (mostly free): prompt engineering, RAG, building
  systems with the ChatGPT API, agentic AI. Browse:
  [short courses](https://www.deeplearning.ai/short-courses/)
- **Practical Deep Learning for Coders** — fast.ai (free): top-down deep learning.
  [course.fast.ai](https://course.fast.ai/)

## 🧭 How to choose when overwhelmed

1. **Follow the daily card.** It already tells you the exact resource for today.
2. Use **one primary** resource per topic. Extras (like 3Blue1Brown) are for when
   something doesn't click — not to watch everything.
3. Finish for the **certificate** only when the learning is done — the skill is the
   point; the certificate is the bonus.

## ⚠️ Free-access note (Coursera Plus coverage in Egypt)

**Coursera Plus does NOT cover every course in Egypt.** Some courses (e.g., *Generative
AI for Everyone*) show a paid price even with an active Plus subscription, and may not
offer a free Audit option — only slow (~15-day) Financial Aid.

**Our policy going forward:** learning is never blocked by money.
- Every lesson should have a **free primary option**; Coursera is used only where it's
  actually included/auditable for you, or where a certificate genuinely matters.
- **Skip Financial Aid for short intro courses** — use a free equivalent instead. Save
  Financial Aid only for a big flagship certificate you truly want.

**Reliable free go-to sources** (lean on these when Plus falls short):
- Google Cloud Skills Boost (free) — [cloudskillsboost.google](https://www.cloudskillsboost.google)
- freeCodeCamp (free) — [freecodecamp.org](https://www.freecodecamp.org) & their YouTube
- Hugging Face courses (free, with certificates) — [huggingface.co/learn](https://huggingface.co/learn)
- Andrej Karpathy, 3Blue1Brown, fast.ai — all free (see lists above)

**To verify before Phase 1:** confirm whether **AI Python for Beginners** (the Phase 1
anchor) is Plus-covered / auditable for you. If not, the free backbone is
**freeCodeCamp's "Python for Beginners"** + **py4e.com** — both excellent and free.
